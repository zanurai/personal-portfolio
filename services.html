My Services

Services

U&ME Dance is dedicated to bringing the fun and challenge of dancing to Bellingham and Whatcom County at an affordable cost.

Private Lessons

Private Lessons offer students the best value for their investment. With a private lesson, the students will work one-on-one with an instructor, receiving personalized instruction and immediate correction.

Group Classes

Group Classes are both an introduction to the experience of dancing with different partners, and a chance for the students to become familiar with a variety of step patterns. Rotation of partners, throughout the class allows dancers to practice steps with different partners.

Practice Dances

Practice Dances will be held once a week to allow students an opportunity to practice the dances learned, in an environment with other students learning the same thing.
